BARAKAH KIDS RACE - FEATURE LIST

CORE APP STRUCTURE
- React Native mobile app with Expo SDK 54
- File-based routing with Expo Router
- Zustand state management with AsyncStorage persistence (auto-saves with 300ms debounce)
- 6 bottom tabs: Today, Tasks, Spin, Leaderboard, Rewards, Setup

ONBOARDING FLOW
- Welcome screen introducing the app
- Add family members (kids and guardians) with name, age, and role
- Power selection for kids (1-2 powers each): Organizer, Fast Cleaner, Kitchen Helper, Study Coach, Calm Peacemaker, Discipline
- Task template review with 40+ starter tasks across 7 categories

FAMILY MANAGEMENT
- "Act As" selector to switch between family members
- Add new kids/guardians from Setup tab
- Member Detail screen showing profile, powers, open tasks, and completion history

TASK SYSTEM
- 7 Categories: Cleaning, Kitchen, Learning, Kindness, Prayer, Outdoor, Personal
- 40+ Starter Tasks: Real daily chores like "Make your bed", "Set the table", "Read for 15 minutes"
- Task Templates: Add, edit, archive (soft-delete), enable/disable tasks
- Task Assignment: Guardians assign tasks to kids with due dates
- Task Completion with Approval Workflow:
  - Marking complete sets status to "Pending Approval"
  - A different person must approve or reject
  - Stars only awarded after approval
  - "Needs Approval" section on Today tab

SPIN GAME
- Spin Queue: Stage tasks before spinning
- Quick-create tasks or add from templates
- Spin picks random kid with fairness weighting (reduces repeat winners)
- Proposal Flow: Shows selected kid + task, requires parent approval
- Reroll or Accept options
- Creates TaskInstance only when accepted

STARS & LEADERBOARD
- Kids earn stars for completing approved tasks
- Table-style leaderboard with Rank, Name, Stars (trophy icons for top 3)
- Star Deductions: Guardians can deduct stars for misbehavior
  - Requires a reason to be entered
  - Tracks who made the deduction and when
  - Deduction history visible on Member Detail screen

REWARDS SYSTEM
- Parents set reward goals with star costs (5/10/20/50 or custom)
- Kids see progress bars toward closest reward
- Guardians grant rewards when kids have enough stars
- Stars deducted on redemption
- Claimed rewards history preserved

ISLAMIC VALUES TONE
- Positive, gentle wording throughout
- Sounds disabled by default
- No music autoplay
- Focus on good deeds and family harmony
